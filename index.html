Parametric + Craft Sculptural Design System

Parametric + craft sculptural design system is a research to develop a parametric + craft sculptural design system to author geometric and organic sculptural forms as a part of large immersive augmented reality installations. The necessity of research is to develop tools that combine manual creation with automated generation simultaneously, in order to respond to more computationally-mediated environments where virtual and physical spaces increasingly overlap.

Parametric + craft sculptural design system is aimed to be a more general tool to serve other artsits who are intended to generate digital 3D forms, based on dual approaches: geometric yet organic, and parametric as well as manual, which can then be prototyped into physical sculptures.

The research is funded by OCAD University Partnership Seed Grant and performed by Haru Hyunkyung Ji and Andrew Sidsworth.
