Parametric + Craft Sculptural Design System

Parametric + craft sculptural design system is a research to develop tools that combine manual creation with automated generation simultaneously, in order to respond to more computationally-mediated environments where virtual and physical spaces increasingly overlap. This was originally initialized to author geometric and organic sculptural forms as parts of large immersive augmented reality installations - Artificial nature: Archipelago. However, the parametric + craft sculptural design system is aimed to be a more general tool to serve any artsits who want to generate digital 3D forms, which can then be prototyped into physical sculptures.
